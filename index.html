Who She Is

About Dr. Ella

Dr. Ella Chioma Ezeadilieje — popularly known as "Veronica's Daughter" — is a prominent Nigerian academic, storyteller, and social media influencer born in Jos and originally from Akpo, Anambra State.

She is widely recognised for her advocacy for women's empowerment and her ability to weave heartfelt, relatable stories about life, marriage, and personal growth. Through her digital platform, she has built a large community where she shares insights on identity, purpose, and authentic living.

Her work bridges academia and entrepreneurship — helping women and young professionals build sustainable businesses while maintaining personal well-being.
